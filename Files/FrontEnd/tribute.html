Osagyefo Dr. Kwame Nkrumah

The man who saved a billion lives.

A time line of Dr. Nkrumah's life.

  • 1909: Kwame Nkrumah is born on September 21 in Nkroful, Gold Coast (now Ghana).

  • 1935: Nkrumah moves to the United States to pursue higher education, attending Lincoln University in Pennsylvania.

  • 1945: Nkrumah obtains a Bachelor of Arts degree and later completes a Bachelor of Sacred Theology at Lincoln University.

  • 1947: Nkrumah moves to London, where he studies at the London School of Economics and Political Science, earning a Bachelor of Science in Economics.

  • 1947: Nkrumah becomes involved in the Pan-African movement and advocates for the independence of African nations.

  • 1949: Nkrumah forms the Convention People's Party (CPP) in Ghana, with the goal of achieving independence from British colonial rule.

  • 1951: The CPP wins a majority in the legislative assembly elections, and Nkrumah becomes the leader of government business.

  • 1960: Ghana becomes a republic, and Nkrumah becomes the first President of Ghana.

  • 1964: Nkrumah introduces a one-party state in Ghana and becomes the leader of the only legal political party, the CPP.

  • 1963: Nkrumah advocates for African unity and plays a key role in the formation of the Organization of African Unity (OAU), now known as the African Union (AU).
